## Notes For Future Localization

- Prefer short button labels over literal full-sentence translations.
- Keep mixed English terms when they are common in the target context, such as STL, JSON, CSV, URL, SEO, and API.
- Review Traditional Chinese wording separately. Do not rely on automatic Simplified-to-Traditional conversion.
- Tool names can remain partly English when the English term is the standard user-facing term.
